# Eco-Game
How to Play
Monitor Stats: Track your money and comfort levels using the progress bars at the top of the screen.
Read Scenarios: Review the weather conditions or random events presented in the main card.
Make Choices: Click an option button to take an action based on your current budget and comfort needs.
Check the Log: Watch the event log at the bottom to see your previous actions and costs.

Survive 3 Days: Keep both stats above zero until the end of Day 3 to win the game.
